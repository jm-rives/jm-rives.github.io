About me




Joanna M. Rives is living her third life as a junior software engineer in the wonderland of the Pacific Northwest! She has made a home with her amazingly talented and kind partner and their three cats. She believes that programming is an act of careful and consider craft best done in community. Her preferred pronouns are she/her they/them and it makes her day to be introduced as Mx. Rives.
