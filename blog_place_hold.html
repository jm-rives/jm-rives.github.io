Reflections on the path of programming


"Dear Kitty Timer" is a variation on Rubber Duck Debugging in which explains a problem, in detail, to an inanimate object of choice with the goal of gaining clarity and insight into a a challenge.

Dear Kitty Timer

Technology and programming have always fascinated me. As a child I enjoyed dismantling small appliances to see how they worked and experimenting with the few Macintosh desktops that our school had available. I tinkered with a hand-me-down Timex Sinclair ZX81 even though it was missing a cassette deck to store my programs on and my parents couldn’t afford the replacement. As an adult, I’ve found my interest in programming renewed as I’ve witnessed how programming provides dynamic and powerful tools to conquer the problems I most care about.
